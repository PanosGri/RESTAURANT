Location

On the top of a hill of 370 meters high, of the pure Mani countryside, where prickly pears and herbs have conquered even olive trees vital space, having a view of the whole Laconic gulf.

In the center of our village"Drosopigi" or "Tserova" as it used to be called.

Just take one of the roads from the main road Areopoli-Gythion and follow the signs to Drosopigi.
Via Vachos village,via Neochori village or via Skoutari village
come and find us in the main square.
